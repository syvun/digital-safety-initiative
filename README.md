# CyberGlen: Digital Safety Initiative 

Welcome to the official repository of the **CyberGlen Digital Safety Initiative**.

CyberGlen is a registered **Social Purpose Corporation (SPC)** based in Washington State. Our mission is to strengthen community resilience by creating accessible, free, and easy-to-use digital safety resources for those who need them most.

---

## Our Mission
We are developing a comprehensive series of printable handouts and digital guides specifically designed to protect:
* **Immigrants and newcomers**
* **Senior citizens**
* **Survivors of domestic violence**
* **Non-native English speakers**
* **Other individuals at risk of digital exploitation**

---

## Community Contributors
This is an **Open Source** project. We believe that community safety is a collective effort, and we invite professional and student contributors to help us build these resources.

### How You Can Help:
* **Security Advocates:** Help us draft and review technical content, simplifying complex topics (MFA, Phishing, Device Hardening) for non-technical audiences.
* **Multilingual Contributors:** Use your language skills to translate guides into multiple languages, ensuring inclusivity for all community members.
* **Document and Layout Designers:** Help us format clean, professional, and easy-to-print PDF handouts.
* **Community Researchers:** Identify and document current digital threats and scams targeting vulnerable populations.
* **Open Contributions:** We welcome any expertise that can strengthen this initiative—whether you are a **Technical Writer**, **Project Coordinator**, **Quality Assurance** enthusiast, or simply someone passionate about community safety. If you have an idea on how to improve these resources, we want to hear from you!
