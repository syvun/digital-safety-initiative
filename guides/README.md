# Community Digital Safety Guides

This folder is the core of what we do. We’ve split everything into sub-folders based on different life situations and the groups of people we’re helping. It’s way easier to find what you need when it’s sorted by who you are and what you’re dealing with.

### How we keep things simple:

* **No tech talk.** We use everyday language. If we can explain it without a confusing IT term, we will.
* **Keep it snappy.** These aren't manuals. They are short, "read-in-a-minute" guides that get straight to the point.
* **Focus on the "How."** We don't just talk about problems; we give you the steps to fix them. Just clear, practical advice.
* **Actually helpful.** We know life can be stressful. Our goal is to help you feel safe and in control, not more confused.
* **Built for scanning.** We use lots of bullet points and big headers so you can find the answer you need in a few seconds.

We’re always adding new categories and keeping things updated, so keep an eye on this space.
