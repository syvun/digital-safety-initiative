# How to Participate in CyberGlen 

Thank you for your interest in the CyberGlen Digital Safety Initiative. We believe that community safety is a collective effort and welcome anyone who wants to contribute their professional skills to this mission.

###  Areas for Contribution:
* **Core Resources:** Help develop, refine, or review safety guides and technical content.
* **Localization & Inclusivity:** Help us reach diverse communities by adapting resources into different languages.
* **Visuals & Accessibility:** Help improve the design, layout, and readability of our materials.
* **Information Gathering:** Help identify emerging digital threats and scams targeting the community.
* **General Support:** We welcome any expertise—whether technical, organizational, creative, or otherwise—that can strengthen this initiative. If you have a skill or an idea that can help, we want to hear from you.

### 📩 How to start:
If you would like to participate, please reach out directly via email at: oxyvun@gmail.com or message me on LinkedIn Oksana Syvun.

Please let us know:
1. What skills you’d like to share.
2. What part of the initiative interests you the most.

###  Legal Terms of Participation:
By participating in this project, you agree to the following:

* **Pro-bono Basis:** All contributions are made strictly on a pro-bono, unpaid basis.
* **No Employment:** This participation does **NOT** create an employer-employee relationship, an internship, or a promise of future employment with **CyberGlen SPC**.
* **Ownership & Licensing:** Any work shared here will be released under the project's **CC BY-NC-SA 4.0** license to ensure it remains a free resource for the community.
* **Independent Participation:** Participants contribute at their own pace and discretion, with no mandatory hours, deadlines, or management-led commitments.
